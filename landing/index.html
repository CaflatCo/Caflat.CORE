FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Does it work offline?

Yes — 100% offline-first. No internet connection is required to run daily operations. Data stays on your device and syncs when you're back online (Cloud plan).

What devices does it run on?

Any modern browser on a tablet, laptop, or desktop. Caflat.CORE is optimized for counter use on a dedicated device, but works wherever you have a browser.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — the Free plan is permanently free, no credit card required. It includes core POS and inventory so you can run real operations before committing to a paid plan.

Do I need to install anything?

No installation needed. Open Caflat.CORE in a browser and you're running. Works as a PWA — you can optionally add it to your home screen for an app-like experience.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data is yours. You can export everything — products, transactions, inventory — anytime before you leave. We don't hold your data hostage.

Can I migrate from my existing system?

Yes. We support CSV import for products and inventory, and our team can help you migrate from most POS systems during onboarding.
