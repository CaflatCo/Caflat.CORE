Full traceability, from farm to cup

Know exactly where every gram came from. Scan a coffee bag label with your phone camera — OCR reads the text and auto-fills your lot record in seconds.

  • Lot tracking with farm, farmer, region, variety & process
  • Photo attachments — bag, farm, and label photos on each lot
  • Printable QR labels your own lots can be scanned back in
  • Full trace view linking lots to batches, recipes, and orders
