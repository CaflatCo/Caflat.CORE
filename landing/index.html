Origin Mode

Full traceability, from farm to cup

Know exactly where every gram came from. Scan a coffee bag label with your phone camera — it reads the text and auto-fills your lot record in seconds. Attach photos, link batches, and print QR labels for your own bags.

  • Camera label scanner — photograph any bag to auto-fill
  • Lot tracking: farm, farmer, region, variety, and process
  • Photo attachments — bag, farm, and label shots on each lot
  • Printable QR labels that scan right back into the app
  • Full trace view linking lots to batches, recipes, and orders
